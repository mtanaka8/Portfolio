The Ground Higher

Background

My task as a Graphics Intern was to create Magazine Layouts for possible cover ideas for The Ground Highers first Annual Fundraiser.

Problem

The client has requested the layouts to include a “Tropical Hawaiian Theme.” I began to research possible cover images to utilize for my process. Through Adobe Indesign, I’ve put together two possible covers for the Fundraiser. Howewver, these drafts needed some revisions..

Solutions

After receiving feedback, I’ve began my revisions which led to creating the final version of The Ground Higher’s First Annual Fundraiser Pamphlet Layout.
